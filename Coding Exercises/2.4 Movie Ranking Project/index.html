My Top 3 Tamil Movies of All Time

Vikram

A high-octane action thriller with outstanding performances, intense storytelling, and a powerful cinematic universe.

96

A beautiful and emotional love story that captures nostalgia, friendship, and the pain of lost love.

Soorarai Pottru

An inspiring tale of ambition, perseverance, and determination that motivates you to chase your dreams.

My Top 3 Malayalam Movies of All Time

Drishyam

A masterfully crafted thriller with brilliant twists and one of the most engaging storylines ever made.

Premam

A heartwarming coming-of-age romance that perfectly portrays love, friendship, and life's different phases.

Manjummel Boys

A gripping survival drama based on true events, showcasing friendship, courage, and resilience.
